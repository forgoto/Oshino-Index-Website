This page explains how Oshino Index is intended to be used, what bot-related data may be stored, and how users or server owners can ask questions about removal or support.

Last updated: 16 June 2026

01

Privacy Policy

Oshino Index is a Discord bot built around playful server interaction, character-based progression, collectible items, fortunes, and light utility features. To make these public features work, the bot may store limited Discord-related data.

Data the bot may store

  • Discord user IDs used for profiles, progression, inventory, cooldowns, effects, and command-related records.
  • Discord server IDs and basic server-related information needed for bot operation and maintenance.
  • Gameplay and progression records such as coins, offerings, pats, trust, fortunes, items, active effects, tutorial progress, and related state.
  • Opt-in preference records for supported bot announcements.
  • Limited technical, safety, or moderation-related records used to protect the bot from spam, abuse, exploitation, or unsafe use.

Why this data is used

  • To remember user progression, coins, items, trust, cooldowns, and active effects.
  • To support offerings, fortunes, inventory features, tutorials, and other public bot interactions.
  • To remember opt-in announcement preferences where supported.
  • To protect the bot from spam, abuse, exploitation, or unsafe use.
  • To help maintain bot reliability, troubleshoot issues, and improve user support.

What is not sold

Oshino Index does not sell Discord user data, server data, gameplay records, progression records, or safety-related records.

02

Terms of Use

By inviting or using Oshino Index, you agree to use the bot in a reasonable and respectful way. Server owners and administrators are responsible for deciding whether Oshino Index is suitable for their community.

Acceptable use

  • Do not use Oshino Index to harass, spam, exploit, or disrupt Discord servers.
  • Do not attempt to abuse cooldowns, progression systems, economy features, item systems, or command interactions.
  • Do not attempt to access restricted bot functionality, private systems, or non-public areas without permission.
  • Do not use the bot to store or distribute illegal, harmful, or abusive content.

Bot access and moderation

Access to Oshino Index may be limited, removed, or blocked if abuse, exploitation, spam, or unsafe use is detected. The bot creator may restrict access, disable features, or remove the bot from a server when needed.

Availability

Oshino Index is provided as a personal/community project. Features may change, break, be renamed, be removed, or become unavailable without advance notice. No uptime guarantee is provided.

03

Data & Compliance

Oshino Index is designed to store only the information needed for bot features, safety, and maintenance. The public website itself is static and does not include a login system, server-side forms, or public access to private bot records.

Storage and access

  • Bot-related data may be stored privately for operation, safety, support, and maintenance.
  • Access to private bot records is limited to authorised maintenance needs.
  • Internal maintenance actions may be logged where needed to support reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

Retention

Some data may be kept for as long as needed to provide bot features, maintain records, investigate abuse, restore state, or support server administration. Records may be deleted or anonymised when they are no longer needed or when a valid removal request is accepted.

04

Requests & Contact

Users or server owners may request support, ask what kind of data is stored, or request deletion of bot-related records. Some records may need to be retained temporarily for safety, audit, abuse-prevention, or technical reasons.

How to contact

  • Use the support page on this website.
  • Contact the creator on Discord: @forgotten.wasurerareta.
  • Use !mbug in Discord for bug-report guidance where available.

Helpful information for requests

  • Your Discord user ID.
  • The server ID, if the request is server-related.
  • The command or feature involved, if relevant.
  • A clear description of what you want reviewed or deleted.
05

Disclaimer

This page is written in plain language for transparency and project governance. It is not formal legal advice. The bot creator may update this page as Oshino Index changes, especially if new public features, storage needs, safety controls, or support processes are added.
